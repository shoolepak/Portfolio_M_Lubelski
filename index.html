Coding

As for my coding examples, this website might be one of them! Some of my more notable projects include a voxel game engine prototype written in Python, my work on a VR art exhibition in Unity with C#, and various data analysis projects I developed for university, personal use, and work.

I have experience coding in C++, C#, HTML, JavaScript, and Python. Of these, I would say I know Python and HTML the best.

The voxel game I mentioned features infinite world generation‚Äîboth horizontally and vertically. As you dig, the world generates beneath you dynamically to optimize performance. It also includes a speed-building system (activated by holding Shift) and a save/load function for preserving progress.

On the right, you can see footage from my prototype voxel game and the VR app for Hues of Happiness, an exhibition held at the University of Guelph-Humber in Toronto.
